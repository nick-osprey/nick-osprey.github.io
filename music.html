Music Projects

Other than technology, music is one of my biggest passions. I've been playing guitar since I was 10 years old and play almost everday. I love experimenting with different styles and genres but I mostly prefer a sound that's folkier. Since I was in highschool I've been self publishing my own recordings on sites like Bandcamp under various different names. My two main projects are Nickle Pickle and Blueberry-Lemon, the latter being for older songs I've recorded.

My latest release 'The Osprey' was written in memory of my Grandmother. The song is about an osprey flying away toward the sunset.

This song is a more experimental ambient piece which heavily utilies looping and delay effects to create a calm atmosphere.

This one was recorded during a jam session between my brother and I at his place in a rainy Dartmouth evening. Thus the name 'Dartmouth'. One of my earliest recordings from way back in 2017. The picture is of my cat Ted.

My first full album release. Consists of various recordings taken throughout the years.
